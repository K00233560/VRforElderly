About VR for the Elderly

For people who may be housebound due to limited mobility or illness, the real world can become very small and stifling. VR for the Elderly uses 360-degree virtual reality to help older adults to overcome this problem. 360-degree virtual reality is a computer-generated audio-visual simulation of an environment that surrounds a user. Using a head-mount display, the user can view the environment from all directions, just as they can in real life. VR for the Elderly enables older adults to immersive themselves into the outside world, from the comfort of their own home.
